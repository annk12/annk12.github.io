How to make
strawberry cake

1.Preheat oven and prepare cake pans.
2.Sift cake flour and whisk dry ingredients.
3.In an electric mixer cream butter and sugar.
4.Mix in eggs and yolks separately then blend in vanilla.
5.Blend in flour mixture alternating with additions of milk.
6.Divide among cake pans and bake until set.
